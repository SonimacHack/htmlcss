Sapang Dalaga

Sapang Dalaga is a quiet coastal town in the northern part of Misamis Occidental. The placid waters of Casul Bay hems its shore while Mount Dasa & Pedoluan frames it on land. As the town thrives mainly on agri & aqua cultures, fresh vegetable & seafood meals are guaranteed gastronomic treats.

Ozamis

Ozamiz is a coastal component city in the province of Misamis Occidental. The city has a land area of 169.95 square kilometers or 65.62 square miles which constitutes 8.47% of Misamis Occidental's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 140,334.

Bonifacio

Bonifacio is a landlocked municipality in the coastal province of Misamis Occidental.The municipality has a land area of 155.02 square kilometers or 59.85 square miles which constitutes 7.73% of Misamis Occidental's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 34,558. This represented 5.60% of the total population of Misamis Occidental province, or 0.69% of the overall population of the Northern Mindanao region.
